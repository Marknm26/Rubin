Breed

The Rubin dog breed is a delightful and energetic breed that shares many characteristics with the well-known Miniature Pinscher but with its unique twists. This breed stands out with its vibrant, ruby-red coat that glistens in sunlight, a feature that gives the Rubin its name. They are small but sturdy, typically weighing between 8 to 12 pounds, and their sleek body is often adorned with delicate white markings on the paws and chest.

Nature

Rubins are renowned for their alert and spirited nature. They are exceptionally curious, always eager to explore their surroundings, and have an abundance of energy, which makes them excellent companions for active families. Despite their small size, they are fearless and possess a confident demeanor that belies their stature
